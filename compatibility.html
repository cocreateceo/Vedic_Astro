About Ashtakoot Guna Milan

The traditional Vedic method analyzes 8 aspects of compatibility

Varna (1 Point)

Spiritual compatibility and ego levels. Measures the basic matching of temperaments.

Vashya (2 Points)

Mutual attraction and control in relationship. Determines power dynamics between partners.

Tara (3 Points)

Destiny and luck compatibility. Indicates health and well-being of the relationship.

Yoni (4 Points)

Physical and sexual compatibility. Important for intimacy and mutual satisfaction.

Graha Maitri (5 Points)

Mental compatibility and friendship. Based on Moon sign planetary rulers.

Gana (6 Points)

Temperament matching. Classifies as Deva, Manushya, or Rakshasa temperament.

Bhakoot (7 Points)

Emotional compatibility and family welfare. Affects prosperity and children.

Nadi (8 Points)

Health and genetic compatibility. Most important factor for progeny and health.
